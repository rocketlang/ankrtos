## Presentation Tips

### **Before the Meeting:**
1. Send slides 24 hours ahead
2. Test all demos
3. Prepare backup (no internet scenario)
4. Have printed handouts ready

### **During Presentation:**
1. Speak slowly and clearly
2. Pause after each slide for questions
3. Use real examples (Priya's story)
4. Show enthusiasm but stay professional
5. Listen more than you talk

### **Body Language:**
1. Make eye contact
2. Smile genuinely
3. Use hand gestures (moderate)
4. Stand/sit with confidence
5. Show you believe in the mission

### **Handling Questions:**
1. "Great question!" (acknowledge)
2. Answer honestly (if you don't know, say so)
3. Write down questions for follow-up
4. Don't get defensive
5. Circle back to student impact
