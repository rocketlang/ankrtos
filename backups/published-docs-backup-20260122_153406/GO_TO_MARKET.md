## Launch Strategy

### Week 1: Soft Launch
**Goal:** 1,000 users, validate product-market fit

**Tactics:**
- ProductHunt launch
- HackerNews Show HN post
- Reddit (r/privacy, r/degoogle)
- Personal networks

**Content:**
- Launch blog post
- "Why I Built ankrshield" founder story
- Video demo (2 min)

### Week 2-4: Momentum Building
**Goal:** 10,000 users

**Tactics:**
- Press outreach (TechCrunch, The Verge, Ars Technica)
- Privacy influencers (YouTube, Twitter)
- Content marketing (blog posts)
- Community engagement

**Content Ideas:**
- "How [Popular App] Tracks You"
- "I Monitored ChatGPT for 7 Days. Here's What It Did."
- "Pegasus Spyware: How to Detect It"

### Month 2-6: Growth
**Goal:** 100,000 users

**Channels:**
1. **Organic (40%)**
   - SEO blog content
   - YouTube tutorials
   - Open source community
   - Word of mouth

2. **Paid (30%)**
   - Google Ads (privacy keywords)
   - Reddit ads
   - Podcast sponsorships
   - Twitter ads

3. **Partnerships (20%)**
   - VPN providers (NordVPN, ProtonVPN)
   - Password managers (1Password, Bitwarden)
   - Privacy tools (Signal, Tor)

4. **PR (10%)**
   - Tech publications
   - Privacy advocates
   - Conference talks

## Pricing Strategy

**Free Tier:**
- Unlimited to drive adoption
- 1 device limit encourages upgrade

**Premium ($9.99/month):**
- Unlocks key features
- Target: 10% conversion

**Messaging:**
- "Your coffee costs more than your privacy"
- "Less than $0.33/day"
