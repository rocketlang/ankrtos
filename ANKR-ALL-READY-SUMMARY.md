## ğŸ¯ What We're Building (Summary)

### **Three Platforms:**

1. **ANKR EDU** (Months 1-6)
   - For K-12 students & teachers
   - Pratham focus
   - Q&A, audio lessons, quizzes, progress tracking

2. **ANKR RESEARCH** (Months 7-9)
   - For researchers & professionals
   - Multi-document analysis, literature reviews
   - Monetization to sustain free EDU version

3. **ANKR OPEN** (Months 11-12)
   - Open source, self-hosted
   - Community-driven
   - Free forever

### **Core Technologies:**

- **RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation):** Smart Q&A from documents
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol):** AI tools integration
- **Ollama:** Local AI (FREE, privacy-first)
- **pgvector:** Vector similarity search
- **edge-tts:** Free text-to-speech

### **Key Innovations:**

1. Knowledge Graphs (visual learning)
2. Study Packages (complete materials in one click)
3. Multi-source Intelligence (ask across multiple books)
4. Weak Area Detection (AI finds what students struggle with)
5. 22 Indian Languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, etc.)
6. Offline-first (works without internet)
