SUBJECT: PORT OPERATIONS AND BERTHING PROCEDURES - SINGAPORE PORT

1. PORT CONGESTION ALERT
Current Status: MODERATE CONGESTION
- Container terminals: 2-3 days waiting time
- Bulk terminals: 1-2 days waiting time
- Tanker berths: Normal operations
- Anchorages: 85% occupancy

Vessels are advised to confirm berth availability 72 hours prior to arrival.

2. ANCHORAGE AREAS
Eastern Anchorage (EA):
- Available berths: 45
- Current occupancy: 38 vessels
- Suitable for: Bulk carriers, general cargo
- Depth: 15-25 meters
- Coordinates: 1°13'N, 104°05'E

Western Anchorage (WA):
- Available berths: 60
- Current occupancy: 52 vessels
- Suitable for: Container vessels, tankers
- Depth: 20-30 meters
- Coordinates: 1°12'N, 103°48'E

3. PILOTAGE REQUIREMENTS
Compulsory Pilotage:
- All vessels over 500 GT
- All vessels carrying dangerous goods
- All vessels with drafts exceeding 10 meters

Pilot Boarding Grounds:
- Eastern Pilot Boarding Ground (EPG): 1°14'N, 104°08'E
- Western Pilot Boarding Ground (WPG): 1°12'N, 103°42'E

Booking Requirements:
- Minimum 4 hours advance notice
- 24 hours for dangerous goods carriers
- 48 hours for LNG/LPG vessels

Contact: Singapore Pilots (24/7)
Tel: +65 6325 2489
Email: pilots@mpa.gov.sg
VHF Channel 12

4. TRAFFIC SEPARATION SCHEME
Singapore Strait TSS:
- Strictly enforce separation scheme
- Speed limit: 12 knots through strait
- No anchoring in traffic lanes
- Report to VTIS on VHF Channel 10

Reporting Points:
- Point Alpha: 1°16'N, 103°50'E (Westbound)
- Point Bravo: 1°10'N, 104°24'E (Eastbound)

5. TERMINAL OPERATIONS
PSA Terminals:
- Tanjong Pagar: Normal operations
- Keppel: Normal operations
- Brani: Normal operations
- Pasir Panjang (Phases 1-4): Normal operations

Jurong Port:
- Bulk terminals: Normal operations
- Steel terminal: 24-hour delays due to equipment maintenance
- Cement terminal: Normal operations

6. BUNKERING OPERATIONS
Designated Bunker Areas:
- EA-1, EA-2, EA-3 (Eastern Anchorage)
- WA-1, WA-2 (Western Anchorage)

Bunker Suppliers:
- Over 50 licensed bunker suppliers available
- Average delivery time: 6-8 hours from order
- Bunker quality testing: Mandatory for all deliveries

Fuel Specifications Available:
- VLSFO (0.5% sulfur) - Compliant with IMO 2020
- LSMGO (Marine Gas Oil) - For auxiliary use
- MGO (0.1% sulfur) - For ECA transit

7. DANGEROUS GOODS REGULATIONS
Pre-arrival Notification:
- Mandatory 48 hours before arrival
- Submit DG manifest to MPA
- Obtain berth assignment confirmation

Segregation Requirements:
- Class 1 (Explosives): Dedicated berths only
- Class 3 (Flammable liquids): Minimum 100m separation
- Class 7 (Radioactive): Special permission required

8. WEATHER AND TIDES
Current Season: Northeast Monsoon
Expected Conditions:
- Wind: NE 15-20 knots, gusts to 25 knots
- Sea state: Moderate, 1.5-2.5 meter swells
- Visibility: Generally good, 5-10 nm
- Rain: Intermittent thunderstorms afternoon/evening

Tidal Information (February 2026):
Spring Tides: Feb 2-5, Feb 16-19
High Water (HW): Up to 3.2 meters above chart datum
Low Water (LW): Down to 0.3 meters above chart datum

9. ENVIRONMENTAL REGULATIONS
MARPOL Compliance:
- Zero discharge of garbage within port limits
- Oily water discharge strictly prohibited
- Reception facilities available at all terminals
- Garbage disposal: Book 24 hours in advance

Emission Control:
- Shore power available at selected berths
- Low-sulfur fuel mandatory (0.5% max)
- Vessels at berth: Minimize auxiliary engine use

10. PORT SECURITY (ISPS CODE)
Security Level: MARSEC Level 1 (Normal)
Requirements:
- Valid ISSC certificate mandatory
- SSP (Ship Security Plan) onboard
- Security drills: Verify before arrival
- ID checks: All crew/visitors

Restricted Areas:
- Military installations: Keep clear 500m
- Petroleum terminals: No photography
- LNG terminals: Enhanced security checks

11. SHIP-TO-SHIP (STS) OPERATIONS
Designated STS Areas:
- Eastern STS Zone: 1°15'N, 104°10'E
- Western STS Zone: 1°11'N, 103°44'E

Requirements:
- Apply 7 days in advance
- Submit STS plan and checklist
- Minimum fender requirements: 6 pneumatic fenders
- Certified STS provider mandatory

12. NAVIGATION WARNINGS
Temporary Restrictions:
- Feb 3-5, 2026: Naval exercises, area 1°20'N to 1°30'N, 104°E to 105°E
  Vessels advised to avoid area or proceed with caution

- Feb 10-12, 2026: Cable laying operations, vicinity 1°16'N, 104°02'E
  Mariners keep clear 500 meters
