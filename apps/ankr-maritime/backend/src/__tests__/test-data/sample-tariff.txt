PORT OF MUMBAI
TARIFF SCHEDULE

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

SECTION 1: VESSEL SERVICES

1.1 Port Dues
    - Vessels up to 10,000 GRT: USD 0.15 per GRT
    - Vessels 10,001 - 50,000 GRT: USD 0.12 per GRT
    - Vessels over 50,000 GRT: USD 0.10 per GRT

1.2 Pilotage
    - Inward/Outward: USD 1,500 base + USD 0.50 per GRT
    - Shifting: USD 800 base + USD 0.30 per GRT

1.3 Towage
    - One tug: USD 2,000 per operation
    - Two tugs: USD 3,500 per operation
    - Additional tug: USD 1,800 per operation

1.4 Berth Hire
    - Container berth: USD 0.08 per GRT per day
    - General cargo berth: USD 0.06 per GRT per day
    - Bulk cargo berth: USD 0.05 per GRT per day

SECTION 2: CARGO SERVICES

2.1 Container Handling
    - 20ft Container: USD 150 per TEU
    - 40ft Container: USD 250 per TEU
    - Reefer Container: Additional USD 100 per TEU

2.2 Bulk Cargo Handling
    - Coal: USD 8 per metric ton
    - Iron Ore: USD 7 per metric ton
    - Grain: USD 10 per metric ton

2.3 Storage
    - First 7 days: Free
    - 8-14 days: USD 5 per TEU per day
    - 15+ days: USD 10 per TEU per day

SECTION 3: OTHER SERVICES

3.1 Fresh Water
    - USD 5 per metric ton

3.2 Garbage Disposal
    - USD 200 per call

3.3 Security Services (ISPS)
    - USD 500 per 24 hours

3.4 Mooring/Unmooring
    - USD 300 per operation

Notes:
- All rates are subject to 18% GST
- Rates are in USD and can be paid in INR at prevailing exchange rate
- Port reserves the right to revise rates with 30 days notice
