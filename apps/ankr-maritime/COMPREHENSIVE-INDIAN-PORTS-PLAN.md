  createdAt DateTime @default(now())
  updatedAt DateTime @updatedAt

  @@index([state])
  @@index([portType])

## ğŸ“Š Tariff Structure by Port Category

### Major Ports (Standard Structure)
- Port Dues: INR 2.40-2.75 per GRT
- Pilotage: 4-5 tiers by size
- Berth Hire: INR 3.50-4.20 per GRT/day (terminal-specific)
- Anchorage: INR 1.20-1.50 per GRT/day
- Towage: Size-based (3-4 tiers)

### Gujarat Minor Ports (Competitive Pricing)
- Port Dues: INR 2.00-2.40 per GRT (20% lower than major)
- Pilotage: Optional at some ports
- Berth Hire: INR 2.80-3.50 per GRT/day
- Simplified structure

### Anchorage Ports (Lightering Focus)
- Anchorage Only: INR 0.80-1.20 per GRT/day
- STS Operations: Additional INR 0.50 per GRT
- No berth hire (anchorage only)
- Pilotage for STS: Lumpsum INR 50,000-100,000

### Captive Ports (Cost-based)
- Port Dues: INR 1.50-2.00 per GRT (cost recovery)
- Limited services (dedicated cargo)
- Often no pilotage (company pilots)
