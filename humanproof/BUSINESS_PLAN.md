# HumanProof - Business Plan

## üéØ Executive Summary

**Problem:** AI-generated content is flooding the internet. There's no reliable way to prove content is genuinely human-made.

**Solution:** HumanProof - A verification platform that certifies human authenticity through challenges AI cannot easily pass.

**Market:** Creators, professionals, platforms, and anyone wanting to prove authenticity in an AI-saturated world.

**Revenue:** Freemium SaaS model + B2B API licensing.

**Vision:** Become the blue checkmark for humanity in the age of AI.

## üìà Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch & Validation (Months 1-3)

**Target:** Early adopters, tech-savvy creators

**Tactics:**
1. **Product Hunt launch**
   - Prepare: Create demo video, testimonials, pitch
   - Goal: Top 5 product of the day
   - Budget: $500 for design/video

2. **Reddit/HackerNews**
   - Post on r/ChatGPT, r/artificial, r/SideProject
   - HN "Show HN" post
   - Focus on contrarian angle: "Fighting AI with AI"

3. **Twitter/X campaign**
   - Target AI skeptics and human authenticity advocates
   - Use hashtags: #HumanMade #NoAI #AuthenticCreation
   - Reach out to anti-AI influencers

4. **Creator communities**
   - DeviantArt, ArtStation (artists)
   - Medium, Substack (writers)
   - GitHub (developers)

**Success Metrics:**
- 500 registered users
- 100 verified certificates issued
- 10 paid subscribers
- $100 MRR

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-12)

**Target:** Professional creators, small platforms

**Tactics:**
1. **Content marketing**
   - Blog: "The Crisis of AI Attribution"
   - Case studies: Early adopter success stories
   - SEO for "human verification" keywords

2. **Integration partnerships**
   - Approach portfolio platforms (Behance, Dribbble)
   - WordPress plugin for verification badges
   - Ghost, Substack, Medium integrations

3. **Influencer marketing**
   - Partner with creators who refuse to use AI
   - Sponsor anti-AI content creators
   - Get featured in AI ethics discussions

4. **B2B outreach**
   - Cold email to hiring platforms (LinkedIn, AngelList)
   - Pitch to art marketplaces (OpenSea, Foundation)
   - Approach content platforms (Medium, Substack)

**Success Metrics:**
- 10,000 users
- 2,000 verified certificates
- 200 paid subscribers ($2,000 MRR)
- 3 B2B pilot customers

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)

**Target:** Enterprise platforms, mainstream adoption

**Tactics:**
1. **Platform integrations**
   - Twitter/X verification badge partner
   - Instagram "Human Verified" badge
   - LinkedIn skill verification

2. **Enterprise sales**
   - Hire sales team
   - Target: Upwork, Fiverr, 99designs, Toptal
   - Pitch: Reduce AI fraud, increase trust

3. **Media & PR**
   - Tech press: TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired
   - Mainstream: NYT, WSJ (AI authenticity angle)
   - Podcasts: AI ethics shows

**Success Metrics:**
- 100,000 users
- $50,000 MRR
- 10+ enterprise customers
- Break-even or profitable
